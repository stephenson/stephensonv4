VP Product

Whimsical

At Whimsical, I lead the product department with the responsibility of Product Management, design and analytics. My main task at Whimsical is to scale the product organisation from a founder-led small team to a large scale product organisation that can deliver high-value outcomes.

Founder and CEO

Legal Monster / Openli

I founded Legal Monster (today called Openli) with my co-founders to help companies comply with a fast-growing set of privacy laws worldwide. I was responsible for the product, tech, investors and overall strategy.

VP Growth

Trustpilot

At Trustpilot, I was a part of product leadership and top management responsible for our freemium model and product adoption on the B2B side. My main focus was building and enabling empowered teams to work towards our commercial goals with a product mindset.

Founder and VP Product

Firmafon / Relatel

I founded Firmafon with my co-founders to make it dead simple for small companies to offer world-class phone support. We did so with our simple online switchboard, 100% SaaS-based solution. Later we added a mobile subscription business on top. The company was subsequently sold to TDC and renamed Relatel. Today with more than 100.000 subscribers.

Side project

SAR Location

SAR Location is a small side project I am building to support the danish maritime search and rescue organisation DSRS locating its casualties. It's a simple product that utilises the GPS built into most smartphones to help locate people at sea.
